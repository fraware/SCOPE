# Limitations

## v0.1 scope

- No web UI or review queue management
- No digital signatures on grants
- No multi-party simultaneous review workflow (co-review flag only)
- Biosecurity and clinical reviewer roles are placeholders
- Local JSONL ledger only (no external tamper-evident store)
- Reviewer identity is caller-supplied, not cryptographically bound

## Non-goals

SCOPE does not replace AKTA, PF-Core, VSA, PCS, IRB, biosafety, EHS, or legal compliance. It does not certify scientific safety or reviewer competence.
